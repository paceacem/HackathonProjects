Thrilling Adventures in Nepal

Paragliding in Pokhara

Soar like a bird over the stunning landscapes of Pokhara with its serene lakes and the majestic Annapurna range as your backdrop.

  • Location: Pokhara
  • Best Time: October - April
  • Speciality: Scenic aerial views of the Himalayas

Visitor Review: "The feeling of flying over Phewa Lake is surreal. An unforgettable experience!"

White Water Rafting

Experience the thrill of conquering roaring rapids in Nepal's rivers, surrounded by lush greenery and towering mountains.

  • Location: Trishuli River, Seti River
  • Best Time: March - June, September - November
  • Speciality: Exciting rapids and stunning scenery

Visitor Review: "An adrenaline-packed adventure with breathtaking views of nature."
