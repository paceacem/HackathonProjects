Explore the Historical Heritage of Nepal

Bhaktapur Durbar Square

Bhaktapur Durbar Square, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a living museum of Newar culture and medieval architecture.

  • Location: Bhaktapur
  • Best Time to Visit: October - March
  • Speciality: Ancient palaces and intricate woodwork

Visitor Review: "The craftsmanship here is awe-inspiring. It feels like stepping back in time."

Lumbini

Lumbini, the birthplace of Lord Buddha, is a sacred pilgrimage site that attracts visitors from around the globe.

  • Location: Rupandehi District
  • Best Time to Visit: February - April, October - December
  • Speciality: Spiritual significance and ancient monasteries

Visitor Review: "A serene place with profound spiritual energy. A must-visit for peace seekers."
