Hidden Natural Gems of Nepal

Rara Lake

Rara Lake, located in the remote Karnali region, is the largest freshwater lake in Nepal. Surrounded by pine forests, it offers breathtaking tranquility and beauty.

  • Location: Mugu District
  • Best Time to Visit: September - November
  • Speciality: Peaceful environment and scenic views

Visitor Review: "An untouched paradise. The serene atmosphere is unmatched."

Phoksundo Lake

Phoksundo Lake is a stunning alpine lake located in Shey Phoksundo National Park. Known for its vivid turquoise waters, it is a hidden treasure of Dolpo.

  • Location: Dolpo Region
  • Best Time to Visit: March - May, September - November
  • Speciality: Turquoise water and cultural richness

Visitor Review: "A magical place with captivating beauty. A must-visit for adventurers."
