Discover Temples of Nepal

Pashupatinath Temple

The Pashupatinath Temple is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most sacred Hindu temples in Nepal. It is located on the banks of the Bagmati River in Kathmandu.

  • Location: Kathmandu
  • Opening Hours: 4:00 AM - 9:00 PM
  • Speciality: Hindu religious significance

Visitor Review: "A mesmerizing spiritual experience! The architecture is stunning."

Swayambhunath Stupa

Swayambhunath, also known as the Monkey Temple, is a famous Buddhist shrine that offers a panoramic view of Kathmandu Valley.

  • Location: Kathmandu
  • Opening Hours: Sunrise to Sunset
  • Speciality: Buddhist heritage and scenic views

Visitor Review: "A serene place with a breathtaking view of the city."
