WELCOME TO NEPAL

Nepal, the land of mountains, is home to breathtaking natural beauty and rich cultural heritage. From Mount Everest to Lumbini, the birthplace of Buddha, Nepal offers a unique blend of adventure and spirituality. Explore its diverse landscapes, ancient temples, and vibrant festivals. Tourism in Nepal is a journey through time, culture, and the heart of the Himalayas.
